There are many defensive items that make pokemon that much harder to defeat.
There is the Eviolite which increases both defense stats of the pokemon that is holding it if they aren't fully evolved.
There is the Leftovers which give the pokemon 1/16th of its health after every turn.
There is the Rocky Helmet which damages the pokemon attacking the holder if they use a physical move.
There are also many berries that increase the bulkiness of the pokemon
