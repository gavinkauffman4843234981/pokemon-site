About Stats

These are the pokemon Raichu's stats, stats are values that determine almost everything about the pokemon.
Special Attack and Attack determine the pokemons damage when using special and physical attacking moves.
Special Defense and Defense determine how much damage the pokemon takes when getting hit by special and physical attacks.
HP (Health Points) determine the maximum ammount of health the pokemon can have.
Speed determines which pokemon goes first, the pokemon with the higer speed stat will always move first unless the other pokemon uses a priority move.

Some pokemon have higher Base Stat Totals which is all of their other stats added together, making these pokemon usually stronger.
Pokemon's stats increase when they evolve with almost all pokemon.
There is also a hidden value called Experience Values, these values are hidden to the player and increase one of the pokemons stats, every pokemon has a ev pool and if you faint them you get 1 or 2 of their ev's.
