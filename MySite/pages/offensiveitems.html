There are many different offensive items in pokemon, there is the Life Orb, which makes the holder lose 10% of their health every turn for a 30% damage increase on their attacks.
There are the "Choice" items, these items raise either speed, attack, or special attack, but the holder can only use the first move they pick when they switch in.
There are Z-crystals which allow the holder to use a Z attack once every battle. There are Plates for every type that increase the damage of that types moves by 20%. There are also Gems that do the same thing as plates but 50% damage and once every battle.
